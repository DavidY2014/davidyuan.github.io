Short Bio:

David Yuan is on track to receive his Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence from Macau University of Science and Technology (MUST) in October 2023. Before embarking on his doctoral journey, David accumulated 4 years of professional experience as a Software Engineer at United Imaging Healthcare (UIH). He holds a Master's degree in Software Engineering from University of Science and Technology of China(USTC) and another in Network Engineering from Nanjing University of Post and Telecommunication(NJUPT).

His primary focus revolves around leveraging AI models to analyze problematic code and detect vulnerabilities, particularly in smart contracts. David's future aspirations include harnessing embedding techniques combined with AI to scrutinize abnormal IoT traffic. His goal is to conceptualize, design, and implement a comprehensive IoT detection system. Over the next 1 to 3 years, he plans to contribute to several conferences and journals in his field of expertise.
